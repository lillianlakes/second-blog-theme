About Me

Welcome to my site, where I share with you some of my favorite international destinations visited so far and those on my bucket list. I'm Lillian Lakes and I'm originally from West Africa. I was born in Liberia, and spent most of my childhood in Nigeria, before coming to the US for college. I've lived in the US for more than twenty years, but enjoy traveling globally. I would like to hear about your experiences in places you've visited. You just might influence my next trip abroad!

Please feel free to read about my adventures in the places I've visited, like posts, leave a comment about your experience in the countries you've visited, and subscribe to my blog. There's so much to explore and discover.

"Twenty years from now you will be more disappointed by the things you didn't do than by the ones you did do. So throw off the bowlines! Sail away from safe harbor. Catch the trade winds in your sails. Explore. Dream. Discover!"
